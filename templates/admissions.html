Join the Future

Take the first step towards a brilliant career. Discover our seamless admission process.

Online Application

Fill out the application form on our official portal. Upload necessary academic documents and identification proofs.

Entrance Test (JET)

Qualify the JIS University Entrance Exam (JET) or submit national-level exam scores like WBJEE/JEE Main.

Counseling & Seat Allotment

Attend the counseling session where seats are allocated based on merit, availability, and eligibility criteria.

Document Verification & Enrollment

Verify original documents physically at the campus and pay the semester fees to confirm your enrollment.
